About the MLS Virtual Lab

The MLS Virtual Lab is a digital solution designed to bridge the practical training gap in Medical Laboratory Science education across Nigeria and beyond.

Why We Built This

Many MLS students graduate without ever handling real lab equipment or participating in live diagnostic workflows. We believe every student deserves hands-on experience, even in virtual form.
